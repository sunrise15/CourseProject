

Smartphone Experiment Tidy Data Set Variables

	Activity
	Activity Code.  What the subject was doing while the observation was taken.
	-	Walking 
	-	Walking Upstairs
	-	Walking Downstairs
	-	Sitting
	-	Standing
	-	Laying

	Variable	
	The type of data that was calculated
	-	Mean
	-	Standard Deviation

	Subject
	The subject performing the experiment
	-	Subject X
	-	Subject Y
	-	Subject Z
	-	ALL – if all subjects were involved

	Average
	Average readings of all observations, for one particular activity, variable, and subject.
